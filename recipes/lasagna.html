Lasagna

A very Italian joy

One of the most famous pasta dishes from Italy (of course). You can choose among different versions of it, according to the tase of the region.

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta sheets, 500g
  • tomato sauce, 1lt
  • mozzarella cheese

Preparation

  2. Put four sheets of lasagna on top
  3. Cover with tomato sauce and mozzarella
  4. Cover with 4 sheets of lasagna
