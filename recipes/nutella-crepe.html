Nutella Crepe

A creamy and warm hug

From France, let's indulge in an evergreen delicacy that no one can turn down. Be it breakfast, lunch, or dinner time.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Nutella

Preparation

  1. Put flour, water, egg and sugar into a bowl an whisk until smooth
  2. Take a large, flat crepe pan on medium fire and wait a couple of minutes until hot
  3. Pour two ladle of the batter on the pan and spread it evenly with the back of a spoon
  4. When it starts to smoke, turn the crepe
  5. Put nutella on top and fold
  6. Put the crepe into a plate and...enjoy!
