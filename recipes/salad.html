Salad

A healthy side when you're done with your main

salad is a simple, quick side you can prepare in no time, helping you reach your five a day. After a succulent lasagna, a salad is what you need to refresh your mouth and call it a meal

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Soft cheese (optional)
  • Salt
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Lemon slice

Preparation

  1. Chop your lettuce in pieces 2 or 3 inches long
  2. Slice your tomatoes
  3. Slice your Cucumber
  4. You can choose to add cheese
  5. Season with salt, a trickle of olive oil and lemon juice to keep up your vit C levels
